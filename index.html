ALMOST HEAVEN

World premiere at the Berlinale Film Festival 2017

Director Carol Salter, 80mins, UK, China, Release date: 2017

A tender elegy to friendship and caring for the dead.

Far from home, afraid of the dark, and terrified of ghosts, Seventeen year old Ying Ling is training to become a mortician in one of Chinaâ€™s largest funeral homes. She learns to perform Spa and beauty treatments to the dead, speaking to them softly, watched by the family members.

Ying Ling also starts forming a special friendship with a young fellow mortician, Jin Hau Together they share the strangeness of working with the dead. When Jin Hau suddenly leaves the funeral home, Ying Ling is left to face the harsh realities of the job alone. Finally she resolves to change her life.
