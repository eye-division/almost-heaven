ALMOST HEAVEN

 

Director Carol Salter

80mins, UK, China

Release date: 2017

 

Showing at the THE BERLINALE FESTIVAL 2017

A tender elegy to first love, friendship and caring for the dead.

 

‘Now you look beautiful and peaceful. I have washed away all your pain and illness. I wish you a good journey. Farewell.’

 

Far from home, afraid of the dark, and terrified of ghosts, Seventeen year old Ying Ling is training to become a mortician in one of China’s largest funeral homes. She learns to perform Spa and beauty treatments to the dead, speaking to them softly, watched by the family members.

 

Ying Ling also starts forming a special friendship with a young fellow mortician, Jin Hau Together they share the strangeness of working with the dead. When Jin Hau suddenly leaves the funeral home, Ying Ling is left to face the harsh realities of the job alone. Finally she resolves to change her life.
